# Project Brief

## Project Name

LUMA Atelier

Luxury Hair, Beauty & Wellness Studio

---

# Business Overview

LUMA Atelier is a premium beauty and wellness destination located in the Netherlands.

The studio combines luxury salon services, holistic beauty treatments, and personalized wellness experiences in a calm, elegant environment.

The brand focuses on quality, craftsmanship, and personalized care rather than high-volume appointments.

Clients visit not only for beauty treatments but for a restorative experience that feels exclusive and deeply personal.

---
